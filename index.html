Thailand Food, Culture & Beach Adventure 2024

Thailand
May 24, 2024 — May 29, 2024
In 0 Days

Your adventure begins in Bangkok, where the air is rich with aromas of street food and the hum of bustling markets. Indulge in a culinary journey through the city, sampling dishes from acclaimed restaurants like Sorn and dining with a view at Blue by Alain Ducasse. After savoring the flavors of this vibrant capital, you will venture north to Chiang Mai, where local farms and heritage will enchant your senses. In Chiang Mai, relax in luxury at 137 Pillars House and discover the region's gastronomy at Khao by Four Seasons. Experience the essence of Thai culture through its food, highlighted by visits to local favorites like Ginger Farm Kitchen. The trip concludes as you reflect on the unique rhythms of life in Thailand, with memories steeped in the tastes and sights of a remarkable journey.
